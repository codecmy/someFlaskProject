A job posting site is an online platform where employers can advertise job openings and connect with potential candidates. These sites allow businesses to reach a wide audience by listing job details such as roles, responsibilities, qualifications, salary, and application deadlines. Job seekers can browse listings based on filters like location, industry, or experience level, and apply directly through the site. Many platforms also offer features like resume uploads, automated job alerts, and AI-based matching to improve the hiring process. Overall, job posting sites streamline recruitment and provide valuable exposure for employers and opportunities for job seekers.
